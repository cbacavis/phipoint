The Phi Peak

The Science of the Peak

The Golden Ratio ($\Phi$) is a mathematical proportion of approximately 1.618. It describes a perfect balance where the small part relates to the large part in the exact same way the large part relates to the whole.

In music, the "Golden Section" occurs at the 61.8% mark of a track's duration.

$$T_{peak} = T_{total} \times 0.618$$
