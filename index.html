    The Science of the Peak

    In mathematics, two quantities are in the Golden Ratio ($\Phi$) if their ratio is the same as the ratio of their sum to the larger of the two quantities. This constant is approximately 1.618.

    When applied to a linear timeline (like a song), the "Golden Section" occurs at the 61.8% mark. In musicology and structural analysis, this point often aligns with the emotional climax or a significant thematic shift.

    $$T_{peak} = T_{total} \times 0.618$$
